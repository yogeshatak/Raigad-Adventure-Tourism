About Raigad

Raigad district in Maharashtra is famous for its historical forts, Sahyadri mountain ranges, waterfalls, beaches, and trekking routes. It is a paradise for adventure lovers and history enthusiasts.

Famous Forts

Raigad is home to many iconic forts built during the Maratha Empire.

Raigad Fort was the capital of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj’s kingdom and stands as a symbol of Maratha pride and bravery.
